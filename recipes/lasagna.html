Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish known for its layers of pasta, meat or vegetable fillings, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is baked to a golden, bubbling perfection, offering a comforting and hearty meal suitable for various occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara or tomato sauce, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, overlapping slightly.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Add a layer of the meat sauce over the ricotta mixture.
  11. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce.
  12. Top with a final layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce.
  13. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese on top.
  14. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Remove from the oven and let the lasagna sit for about 10 minutes before serving.
  16. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve and enjoy!
